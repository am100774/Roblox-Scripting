Basics on Roblox Scripting

1. What is Roblox Scripting?

Roblox scripting uses and coding language called lua.
Lua is just like all the other languages such as Javascript, Python etc.
In this webiste i will tell you how to script in roblox!

The Basics (Coding = Not Required)

So you want to know how to code? First you need to know the basics.
You need to know how to put a part. So you go to to home tab on the top left
click on it and then you will see a button called part. Click on it and a part will
appear on your baseplate!

Properties (Coding = Not Required)

You want to change how the part you just made? We got you. Go to the Explorer
section and select it. Notice that your part is in the section called "Baseplate"
the part you put is the child of the baseplate and the baseplate is the parent of
your part. The baseplate is the child of the "workspace" The Workspace should always
be the parent of everything including the parts, baseplate and even the scripts which we
will get on later. So select the part and then click the "Properties" tab. The tab is in
the Explorer. When you click on the tab, you will be able to change the color and change if
part is collidable or not so test it out!

Building (Coding = Not Required)

If you want to script, you first have to build something to put the scripts into. Or
you could just put the scripts into the baseplate which we will get onto it later.
In the properties tab, click the checkmark next to the "Anchor" option and make sure
there is a blue checkmark. If there isn't, Click on the box next to it. The anchor
option makes the parts stay still and they cannot fall down even if it is in the air.
Try to make a house using parts and also use the color selector in the properties tab.

Script Basics (Printing) (Coding = Required)

Here is when you actually start scripting but before that, you need some essential
tabs open. First, go to the view tab which is next to the plugins tab at the top.
Click on it and then go to the top left and you will see 2 big buttons with icons
called "Explorer" and "Properties". Make sure you have to selected if you dont then
click on them. Then there are some small icons next to them. When you hover over
them, it will tell you what they are and what they mean. Click on the
"Output, Terrian Editor, Toolbox, Command Bar, Script Performance and Script Recovery"
Icons. There! Now you have what you need. Now to create a script you have to go
to Explorer, ServerScriptService click the plus icon next to the ServerScriptService
tab and then press "Script". Now you must have a blank bage and a line of script
called "print("Hello world!"). Remove the Hello World and replace it with anything.
Then add another script saying "print ("Anythingyouwanthere")." Once you are done,
head to the top and click "Run." What it'll do is that it will execute the code you
put in the script and it will appear in the "Output" tab. Nice you learned the
print command. Try to make the print command say about your hobbies or about yourself!
