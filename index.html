Basics on Roblox Scripting

1. What is Roblox Scripting?

Roblox scripting uses and coding language called lua.
Lua is just like all the other languages such as Javascript, Python etc.
In this webiste i will tell you how to script in roblox!

The Basics (Coding = Not Required)

So you want to know how to code? First you need to know the basics.
You need to know how to put a part. So you go to to home tab on the top left
click on it and then you will see a button called part. Click on it and a part will
appear on your baseplate!

Properties (Coding = Not Required)

You want to change how the part you just made? We got you. Go to the Explorer
section and select it. Notice that your part is in the section called "Baseplate"
the part you put is the child of the baseplate and the baseplate is the parent of
your part. The baseplate is the child of the "workspace" The Workspace should always
be the parent of everything including the parts, baseplate and even the scripts which we
will get on later. So select the part and then click the "Properties" tab. The tab is in
the Explorer. When you click on the tab, you will be able to change the color and change if
part is collidable or not so test it out!
